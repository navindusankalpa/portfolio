Final Project of My HND

SmartLiving

- An Arduino Based Home-Automation System -

SmartLiving is an innovative Arduino-based home automation system that integrates seamlessly with an intuitive mobile app to control various devices in your home. With SmartLiving, you can remotely control doors, lights, and receive notifications for crucial events such as gas leaks or rainfall, all through a user-friendly app. The system enhances security, convenience, and safety by providing real-time updates and control over your homeâ€™s environment.

The mobile app allows easy registration of new users, with owners having the ability to add sub-users who have limited access to certain features. While sub-users can interact with basic functions, the owner retains full control, ensuring flexibility and security for everyone in the household. SmartLiving brings smarter living to your fingertips, making home automation more accessible and efficient.
